Overview

Purpose

I love making video games, it is something I want to do for a living. But when I tried to figure out what platform to use out of the many options, it took a good long while for me to find the application I wanted to use. For my website elevator pitch, I would like to make a website that would be a way for people to decide on what game design engine and type of game design they would want to do. It would be an educational website that would include information about different platforms that they can use to learn about what they would be most interested in. Like if they would want to work with 3D projects or 2D projects, what kind of game-making platform they would want to use, and different art platforms to be used as well.

Audience

The audience that would be for this website would be for about 12 years old and up.
